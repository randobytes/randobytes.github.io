About

Welcome to Randobytes!

Hi! My name is Barys, and my journey in game development began back in 1993 in Belarus. It all started when my dad brought home a computer called "Byte" - a localized copy of the famous ZX-Spectrum. That's when I first discovered programming in Basic and Assembler, saving my code to tape and dreaming up pixel worlds.

Randobytes is my indie game studio, a space where I can share some of those dreams with the world. While I'm the sole programmer and game designer behind the games, I don't consider Randobytes a true solo-dev studio. My family's support means everything to me - they've been with me every step of the way. I also collaborate with talented freelancers for music, sound effects, voiceovers, and art. Their contributions are a huge part of what makes these games possible.

Thanks for stopping by - I hope you'll enjoy what Randobytes has to offer!
