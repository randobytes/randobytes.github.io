Crash everything into pixels!

Galaxyz - is a top-down space shooter which combines pixel art graphics with modern effects and an addictive destruction mechanic.

You control a customizable spaceship with blasters and homing missiles navigating endless space, battling other ships, asteroids, explosive objects and dangerous bosses.

The game is fast-paced and designed to be easy to pick up and play, with a focus on the thrill of watching objects break apart pixel by pixel.
