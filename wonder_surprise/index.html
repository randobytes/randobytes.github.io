Galaxyz Wonder Surprise

Big Surprises for Little Learners

Open surprise eggs, collect cute cartoon animals, and learn along the way!

Wonder Surprise is a simple yet exciting game designed for young children. Kids will love the colorful visuals and the joy of discovering new toys inside each egg. While playing, they will also learn animals, numbers, and colors in a playful and engaging way. Start collecting and learning today!

  • Released in: 2019
  • Platform: iOS (iPhone and iPad)

Wonder Surprise key features

  • Open surprise eggs by pulling or tapping - a fun way to learn numbers!
  • Collect adorable cartoon animals and build your own collection.
  • Play again with the toys you've found anytime!
  • Pop soap bubbles for extra fun!
  • Works offline - play anytime, anywhere!
